White Space
Apple
www.apple.com

Apple's website shows us a great example of using the white space on the website. Here the user will find a huge portions of white space surrounding a big image of the product, which will make it more noticeable.

Contrast
Creative Park
www.creativepark.fr

This website is a great example of using contrast princple in the web design. The website's theme is dark and we can also see the product in the middle of the page. The product is only slightly visible because it is black, but the white headline brings user's attention to it.

Repetition
Panic Studio
www.panicstudio.tv

In this website's design repetition is achieved by repeating the logotypes of diffrent companies more than once throughout a design. This section is also dynamic and it makes these brands more reconizable.
